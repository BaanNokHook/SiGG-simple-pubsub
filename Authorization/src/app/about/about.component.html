Have your wallet ready

Make sure you have everything ready to test your wallet (same device and cross device).If needed read our specs.

You must pass both the issue credential and verify credential tests, in this specific order. If you perform both tests, it is important to keep the same conformance header.

Submit a ticket

Once you have passed both tests, you have to create a ticket to submit your conformance header. Note: make sure you have a EU login in order to send us a ticket.

Get the results

The results of the tests will be communicated by the Support Office using the same ticket that you have created in the previous step.
